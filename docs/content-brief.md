> Distilled by the orchestrator from TME's internal strategy vault (company overview deck, operational references, founder history). Claude Code can't see the vault — this is the canonical content source. **Evolve the copy; don't invent facts.** Italian site, professional + warm + efficient tone.

## Brand essence (what every word should serve)
**Efficiency + trust-ability.** TME = *owner-led mission control*: one accountable decision-maker (Ferdinando Martire, Titolare/Responsabile Operativo), **no departmental handoffs**, fast decisions, **door-to-door with no intermediate handoffs**. The promise: **partenza entro 30 minuti**, **24/7/365**, real-time monitoring, email/SMS delivery confirmation, **prezzo tutto compreso e trasparente**. The founder's ethic — relentless, principled, goes to the ends of the earth to do the right thing — *is* the brand. Keep "Affidabilità che conta, velocità che sorprende."

## Positioning upgrade (current site undersells this — but DON'T over-luxe it)
The current copy reads as a generic dedicated-van courier. The reality (from the live dispatch log) is **two registers at once**, and the site must serve BOTH:
- **Everyday & urgent — the bread-and-butter (most jobs).** Anything, anywhere, fast: a forgotten passport/document chased across Italy, a single garment to one person, furniture, dry-cleaning, theatre/event props, industrial parts, restaurant/shop runs, a motorbike across the region. Booked casually (people just phone/WhatsApp to arrange a pickup). **No job too small.**
- **Premium & high-value — the prestige layer (the proof of how good they are).** White-glove handling of fragile, high-value, time-critical goods; fashion/luxury, art & exhibitions, events & hospitality; **OBC / hand-carry by air** for international/critical shipments.

**The line that captures it: "dal singolo capo alla collezione intera" — same care, same 30-minute readiness, whether it's one urgent item or a runway delivery.** Lead with accessibility + reassurance + speed (so the everyday customer feels welcome); let the premium capability and the reviews signal *how trustworthy* they are. **Do not make it read as a luxury-only service** — that scares off the core customer.

### Sectors / use-cases to surface (capability language, NO client names)
- **Privati & urgenze quotidiane** — oggetti dimenticati, documenti, un singolo capo, ricambi, consegne personali urgenti in città, in Italia e all'estero. Partenza entro 30 minuti, nessun incarico troppo piccolo.
- **Aziende & negozi locali** — ritiri/consegne ricorrenti, campionari, materiali, allestimenti, navette inter-sede.
- **Moda & lusso** — trasporti dedicati per sfilate, campionari, capi e accessori di alto valore; **hand-carry in volo** per show internazionali (il servizio premium OBC).
- **Arte & allestimenti** — movimentazione di opere, cornici, materiali d'allestimento per musei/fiere, alta delicatezza.
- **Eventi & hospitality** — logistica per hotel di lusso, eventi, gestione bagagli (stazione/aeroporto di Firenze).
- **Urgenze & spedizioni critiche** — partenza entro 30 minuti, corse nazionali e internazionali quotidiane, monitoraggio in tempo reale.
